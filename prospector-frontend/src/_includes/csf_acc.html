Jobs Accessibility

This map shows the number of jobs that person can access within a 30-minute auto trip or a 45-minute transit trip in 2015 and 2050. This includes jobs location in San Francisco and other counties. These estimates are summarized to Travel Analysis Zones (TAZs) which are spatial units used in travel modeling and analysis.

Use the buttons on the side panel to select the data you want to view. Data selection options include:
  • Year: 2015, 2050, or Change to view the difference between 2015 and 2015.
  • Mode: Auto or Transit
You can also add additional context layers under “Map Layers”.
